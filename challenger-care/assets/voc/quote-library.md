# Voice of Customer · Quote Library

Curated from 480 published JudgeMe reviews · spanning 2007–2026 · 92.5% positive · 65% pomade · 16% shampoo.

This is the ad-ready quote bank. Every quote in here is verbatim, attributable, and scored for specificity + emotional weight + brand-fit. Pull from it for PDPs, ad creative, email subject lines, social posts, and landing pages.

**Migration note:** When the 500 Amazon reviews land, re-run the processing script (`/build/voc/`) — quote rankings will shift, new themes may surface. This doc is a v1, not final.

---

## How to use this library

1. **Pick the theme** that matches the message you're crafting
2. **Borrow the language, not the whole quote** — paraphrase to fit channel + character limits
3. **Always validate against [Claim Library](../knowledge/claim-library.md)** before publishing — quotes can include claims we can't make in marketing copy
4. **Attribution rule:** If using verbatim, attribute to "verified buyer" or first-name only

## What didn't make the cut (and why)

- **"Smells amazing"** quotes — conflict with the brand's fragrance-free positioning · Challenger should not lean on positive-scent reviews
- **"I love it"** without specifics — generic, not ad-ready · violates brand voice rule ("specific claims over poetic ones")
- **Multi-paragraph praise** — too long, can't excerpt cleanly
- **Reviews on retired products** (samplers, discontinued SKUs) — irrelevant to current catalog

## Customer language to borrow (the language bank)

These exact phrases keep showing up. Use them in copy:

- *"Pomade acne"* — the acquisition keyword
- *"All day"* / *"all-day hold"* — the validated hold claim
- *"Actually unscented"* / *"truly fragrance-free"*
- *"Washes out easily"* / *"rinses clean"*
- *"Goes a long way"* / *"a little dab"*
- *"Natural finish"* / *"not greasy"* / *"not shiny"*
- *"Switched from"* / *"used to use"* — switching framing
- *"Won't use anything else"* / *"my new go-to"*

---

## Phrases to AVOID using (off-brand)

From customer reviews — these show up but don't fit the brand voice rules ([Claim Library](../knowledge/claim-library.md)):

- *"Pampers"* / *"nourishing"* / *"hydrating ritual"* (beauty-coded)
- *"Crushes it"* / *"alpha"* / *"boss mode"* (bro-coded)
- *"Self-care"* / *"treat yourself"* (off-brand)
- *"Pampered"* / *"luxurious"* (off-brand)
