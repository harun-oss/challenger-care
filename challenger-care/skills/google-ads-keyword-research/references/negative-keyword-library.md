## How to Apply the Library

**Account-level (all campaigns):**
Add universal negatives + vertical-specific negatives as a Negative Keyword List in Google Ads → Tools → Shared Library → Negative Keyword Lists. Apply the list to all current and future campaigns.

**Campaign-level:**
Apply additional campaign-specific negatives (brand variations, competitor names) directly to individual campaigns rather than the account-level list, since these may not apply to every campaign.

**PMAX:**
Apply the full account-level negative list as campaign-level negatives in PMAX. Also add brand name variations specifically to PMAX to keep branded traffic in the branded RSA campaign.

**Ongoing maintenance:**
Review the Search Terms report weekly for the first 30 days after launch. Add any new irrelevant queries discovered in live traffic to the negative keyword list. Update this library over time with vertical-specific terms discovered through real account data.
